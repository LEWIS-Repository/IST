Natasha Lewis

Natasha has over 19 years of IT experience. She is currently working as an Instructor of Information Systems Technology at Northeast. Prior to that she worked as a Software Engineer at Ashley Furniture and CSA. Natasha holds the following certifications:

  • PearsonVUE Test Administrator
  • CompTIA Security+
  • CompTIA CSA+
  • MTA Software Development Fundamentals
  • PMP
She is also a member of the illustrious Delta Sigma Theta Sorority.
